# Workspace Saver

![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-1.1-blue.svg)
![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-green.svg)

**Never lose your browser worksapce again.**  
A browser extension designed to save, organize, and restore tab sequences even from Private windows.

## ğŸš€ The Problem
When working in a browser especially **Private/Incognito** window, closing the browser means losing all your open tabs instantly. Browser history is disabled, so you can't restore them.

## âœ… The Solution
**Workspace Saver** allows you to snapshot your current tabs into a secure local storage. You can close the browser, come back days later, and restore the exact sequence of tabs with one click.

---

## âœ¨ Key Features

*   **Private Mode Ready:** specifically engineered to persist data locally, bypassing the temporary nature of Incognito mode.
*   **Security Lock:** Protect sensitive workspaces with a custom password.
*   **Drag & Drop Organization:**
    * Reorder your Workspaces list.
    *  Reorder individual tabs inside a workspace.
*   **Smart Restore:** Loads tabs in your *current* window background, keeping your workflow uninterrupted.
*   **Full Control:** Rename workspaces, delete old ones, and edit specific links.



> Toggle/Check the box labeled **"Allow in Incognito"** or **"Allow in Private"**.   
*Without this step, the extension cannot read or save tabs from a Private window.*
